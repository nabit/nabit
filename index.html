How NabIt Works

NabIt itegrates a bookmarklet directly in the users browser. When the user click on the bookmark the url and title information of webpage currently in view is send to NabIt's database. When the client login's in Nabit.com all booksmarks are available to browse.

Limitations

Our bookmarklet utilizes Ajax POST and GET calls to directly transfer and store the client's new bookmook. Unfortunately, on some webpages this may result in something called Cross-Origin conflicts where the website being visited denys Ajax calls to an out-of-domain server. For this reason some pages may not store the bookmark. Nabit incorporates iframe HTML element to render a live presentation of current bookmarks in the user history. Due to a Cross-Origin denial Iframes may also not render an added bookmark.
